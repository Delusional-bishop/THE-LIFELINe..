Services

We offer a variety of services to our donors, including:

  • Blood donation
  • Plasma donation
  • Platelet donation
  • White blood cell donation
  • Apheresis donation

We also offer a variety of support services to our donors, including:

  • Free snacks and drinks
  • Free parking
  • Free Wi-Fi
  • Comfort rooms
  • Donation coaches

We are committed to providing our donors with a positive and rewarding experience. If you are interested in donating blood, please contact us today.
